HR Policy Document - Leave and Benefits

Annual Leave Policy:
All full-time employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave accrues at the rate of 2 days per month. Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days into the next calendar year. Any leave beyond 10 days will be forfeited if not used by December 31st.

Sick Leave Policy:
Employees receive 12 days of sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days. Sick leave cannot be encashed or carried forward.

Maternity and Paternity Leave:
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave. Male employees receive 2 weeks of paid paternity leave. These must be availed within 6 months of the child's birth.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Remote work requests must be submitted at least 24 hours in advance through the HR portal. Employees must be available during core hours (10 AM - 4 PM IST).

Salary Structure:
Base salary reviews are conducted annually in April. Performance bonuses are paid quarterly based on individual and team KPIs. The company matches employee provident fund contributions up to 12% of basic salary.

Health Benefits:
All employees receive comprehensive health insurance covering employee, spouse, and up to 2 children. The annual health insurance coverage is INR 5,00,000. Dental and vision coverage is included. Employees can opt for additional coverage at subsidized rates.
